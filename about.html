About Apex Roofing Co.

Honesty, Integrity, and Craftsmanship.

A Reputation Built on Trust

Mike Harlan grew up in Dallas, swinging a hammer and learning the roofing trade from his uncle starting at age 19. By the time he was 31, he realized the industry needed a change—too many contractors pushing unnecessary replacements and cutting corners on the execution.

That's why he founded Apex Roofing Co. For 18 years, Apex has run entirely on word-of-mouth recommendations and genuine 5-star reviews without ever spending a dollar on traditional advertising.

Mike's promise is simple: If your roof doesn't need to be replaced, he'll tell you. He's turned down work when an honest repair would suffice. That level of integrity is why Dallas homeowners trust Apex when disaster strikes.
